## 🌪️ The Concept
AutoDrive is a **Digital Twin engine** designed for Automotive OEMs to navigate the volatility of global material markets. By mapping complex Bill of Materials (BOM) directly to real-world indices, procurement teams can simulate "What-If" scenarios and visualize the financial impact of geopolitical or energy-driven price shocks.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- **⚡ Live Shock Simulator:** Real-time calculation of component-level price sensitivity based on index fluctuations.
- **🧬 Deep-Tier Mapping:** Visualizing the dependency flow from Tier 2 raw materials to Tier 1 assemblies.
- **📊 Sensitivity Matrix:** Granular breakdown of material compositions (Steel, Copper, Plastics, Semiconductors) for every critical vehicle subsystem.
- **📉 Market Pulse:** Monitoring LME Copper, Brent Crude, SOX Index, and more.

---

## 🛠️ Visual Workflow

### **1. Index Importer**
Adjust global market indices (e.g., LME Aluminum +15%) to see immediate downstream impact.

### **2. Component Impact**
Deep-dive into specific parts like the **Cylinder Block** or **Wiring Harness** to see which specific material is driving their price volatility.

### **3. Network Topology**
A radial graph showing the relationship between the OEM center, key Tier 1 suppliers, and their raw material feedstock.
